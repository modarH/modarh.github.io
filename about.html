Bio

I am a junior programmer who has recently graduated from George Brown College as a Computer Programmer Analyst.

Statement of Career Goal

My personal goal as a developer is to positively affect the life of one person using the power of technology. If someone manages to go through an experience that has made them smile — or allowed them achieve something they otherwise wouldn't have, or even made something they do on a daily basis easier — through an application that I've helped create, then I've achieved my goals as a developer. Or, to keep it concise, my personal goal as a developer is to always strive to make people's lives easier.
